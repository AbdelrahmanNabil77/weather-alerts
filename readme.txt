Developer: AbdElRahman Nabil
Weather forecast app: 
You can find the weather information about your current location for 24 hours in details and for next 7 days, in the home screen, and you can add favorite places to get all the weather information just like the current location, in the favorite screen, and you can an alert to check the weather in a specific date and time. And in the settings screen you can change the temperature and windspeed units, and also you can choose between two languages Arabic and English.
